About This Application

Purpose

This application serves as a comprehensive reference guide for understanding the qualifications required for national-level public officials in the Philippines. It is designed to promote transparency and civic education by making this important information easily accessible.

Importance of Qualifications

The qualifications for public officials are established by the 1987 Philippine Constitution and various laws to ensure that those who serve in government possess the necessary competencies, integrity, and commitment to public service. These requirements help maintain the quality and legitimacy of public governance.

Comprehensive overview of qualifications for Philippine national officials

Coverage

This application covers positions across all branches of government:

  • Executive Branch: President, Vice-President, Cabinet members
  • Legislative Branch: Senators, Representatives
  • Judicial Branch: Supreme Court, appellate courts
  • Constitutional Bodies: Commissions and independent offices

Data Source

All information is based on the 1987 Philippine Constitution and relevant Philippine laws and statutes.
